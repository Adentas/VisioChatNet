About

Our project represents a unique website that integrates artificial intelligence, trained on the famous CIFAR-10 dataset. CIFAR-10 is a standard dataset in the field of machine learning, containing images of various objects such as: cars, birds, cats, deer, frogs, trucks, dogs, horses, airplanes, and ships. Our AI model uses this data to improve its image recognition capabilities, providing accurate and quick results.
